# How handle time with Side Project
Created: 2022-06-06 17:28
Status: #status/permnotes 
Tags: #tags/sideproject   

To organize your time, you need : 
1. organize what you spend your time on (FT work, activities, relaxing)
2. create a schedule 
3. identify free time on's schedule
4. spend 10 minutes, before each session, thinking what youre gonna do.
5. writing things down on paper
6. sometimes you'll need sacrifice to find time

> As a rule of thumb, Only save 5% of what you consume.
