# Is important to have Side Project
Created: 2022-06-06 17:21
Status: #status/permnotes 
Tags: #tags/sideproject 

Side Projects are important for some reasons : 
1. for JR/beginner, your S.P are conversations starters
2. they allow a convo to be natural
3. they can differentiate you from the rest of candidates

Don't force time in your S.P, just need a good organization.
