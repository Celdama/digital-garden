# How finish Side Project
Created: 2022-06-06 17:48
Status: #status/permnotes 
Tags: #tags/sideproject 

Keep in mind : 
- finishing a task is a skill
- you need to develop habit of finishing things
- always keep your focus on the first goal
- sometimes, it's just your brain tricks you and get bored
- setting goals, and checkpoint for your S.P
- can also set an expiration date's S.P
	- after the expiration date, if you're passionate about your S.P, renew the expiration date.
- you want to stop your S.P, it's ok. Just stop it. It's just about to be clear with which projects is in progress and wich are cancelled.
