# Literature Notes
Created: 2022-06-06 10:56
Status: #status/permnotes 
Tags: #tags/PKM #tags/zettelkasten #tags/obsidian

In literature notes, you'll use your fleeting notes and develop your idea with your own words. You can also use directly this type of notes when you read a book, make notes about the content. 
The things to keep in mind is, you have to keep very selective on what you want to select from your book and the most important is, use your own words. 
You have to understand first and write the idea in your own words. 
Keep bibliographical details about your content. 
The main purpose of this type's note is to help with writing [[Permanent Notes]].
