# Permanent Notes
Created: 2022-06-06 10:57
Status: #status/permnotes 
Tags: #tags/PKM #tags/zettelkasten #tags/obsidian 

the idea here is not to collect but to develop ideas, arguments and discussions. You have to use full sentences in your permanent notes, make references between all your notes and be as precise, clear, and brief as possible.
This notes will never be thrown away and contain the necessary information in themselves in a permanently understanble way. In brief, it is theses notes who's add actual value to your system. 
You have to keep this notes atomic, one note can only have one idea and you make references between notes when it's relevant.
