# What is Shiny Dollar Syndrome
Created: 2022-06-06 17:45
Status: #status/permnotes 
Tags: #tags/sideproject

Imagine youre walking down the street. As youre walking, you see a shiny dollar on the ground. You get super excited, so you walk towards it to pick it up. But, before you do, in the corner of you eyes, you see another shinier dollar. So you stop, and then you walk towards the SHINNIER dollar, to pick up. And then it repeats.

Basically, you have spent all this time walking to pick up a dollar, but you end up with no dollars!

This is what happens with S.P, you think of an idea, you get excited, and you start working on it. But then, you get ANOTHER great S.P, then you start working on that one.. repeats.. and repeats..
This syndrome is also valid for learning new langage as JR/beginer.

Shiny Dollar Syndrome can lead to [[Permanent Notes/Tutorial Hell]]
